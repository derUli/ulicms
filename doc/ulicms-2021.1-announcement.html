UliCMS 2021.1 has small detail improvements

For many people 2020 was one of the worst years ever due to the COVID-19 pandemic and freedom and human right limitations that were never there since the second world war.

I hope all UliCMS users got healthy through the winter until yet. This is a rather smaller release. It contains these features and improvements:

  • Favicons can be deleted
  • Logo upload improved
  • Maximum image size can be configured
  • Bootstrap Tooltips
